1.3.1

HIPAA authorization

I am my child’s “personal representative” as that term is used in the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 (HIPAA), 45 C.F.R. § 164.502. I designate my agent as my child’s personal representative while I am incapacitated. I authorize and direct my health care providers, any health plan, any health care clearinghouse, contractor, or other person or entity with information about my child’s health to give my agent full access to that information, and to treat any request from my agent for that health information as though it came from me.
