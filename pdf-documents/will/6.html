Delivery

Anyone receiving tangible personal property under this Part is responsible for the cost of packing, shipping, insuring, and delivering the property.

A gift under this Part includes my rights or proceeds under any insurance policies related to the gifted property.

4.3.5

Survivorship

Unless this will says otherwise, any gift under this Part takes effect only if the beneficiary survives me. If the beneficiary does not survive me, the gift lapses and no anti-lapse rule will apply.
