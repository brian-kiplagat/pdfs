8.3

Indemnification

No executor will be liable for carrying out their responsibilities under this will unless they acted in bad faith, with reckless indifference, or with willful neglect. My estate will indemnify and hold harmless each executor acting under the terms of this will, including reasonable attorneysâ€™ fees for the defense.

8.4

Intent

If I omit anyone from this will, it is on purpose.

8.5

No-contest

If anyone contests or tries to impair this will, I disinherit that person. All gifts given under this will to that person will be forfeited and will augment proportionately the shares of the remainder of my estate distributed to my beneficiaries.

8.6

Savings clause

If any court invalidates any part of this will, the rest continues in effect.

8.7

Taxes

All taxes that result from my death, and all interest and penalties on those taxes, will be paid from the remainder of my estate as an administration expense.
